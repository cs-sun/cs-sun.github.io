Biography

Changsheng is a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Dong Jin Song. His research interests include trustworthy AI, AI safety, and graph neural networks. He holds a Master’s degree from NUS and a Bachelor’s degree from Xidian University. Before his graduate studies, he worked as a Research Intern and Algorithm Engineer at JD Intelligent Cities Research in China.
